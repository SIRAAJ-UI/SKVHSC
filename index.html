Highlights at SKV

We provides the opportunity to prepare for life

Infrastructures

SKV provides uncompromised, best in class infrastructure and facilities for all its students so that nothing is a limitation or challenge for exhibiting their best.

Hostel

Our hostels act as extended homes with good care for each of the students. Exclusive hostels with hygiene and quality are available for girls and boys.

Canteen

SKV has a centralized kitchen housing all the latest cooking and catering appliances. We provide healthy, nutritious diet prescribed by well qualified dietician.

Transport

At SKV we extend access to our school with our well connected transport systems. Safety of your child is ensured while coming to the school and returning home.

Sports

SKV understands and emphasizes on the importance of sports and provide opportunity for the students to practice and excel in a wide range of sports activities.

Medical Assistance

Well-being of our students is of primary importance. At SKV we have a well-equipped medical centre to provide immediate assistance for the students in need.

About SKV

S.K.V School will provide its students with an education by nurturing self-discipline, self-confidence and creativity which will encourage in them an enduring love of knowledge and the passion and sense of improvement, as well as responsibility to make a positive difference in the lives of others through their own actions and example.
