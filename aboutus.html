WELCOME TO SKV

About SKV

SKV was founded by THE VENKANTESWAR TRUST at Tiruchengode, Namakkal District in 2005 by a group of like-minded patroness and educators who had a vision of establishing Model schools that educate and illuminate the future leaders with quality education and to give experiential vision for the young learners to become global citizens in the modern era.

SKV VIDHYAASHRAM is one of the leading CBSE schools in Namakkal District imparting quality education strictly adhering the guidelines prescribed by CBSE, New Delhi since 2012. The school, which had its inception in the year 2005 aimed to give quality and holistic education to the students by adapting various innovative methods of pedagogical process guided by well qualified and experienced facilitators. With the state of the art infrastructure, equipped with innovative teaching gadgets, fully furnished labs and ample rooms for the 360 degree development of the children.  SKVV is considered as one of the best CBSE schools in the Chennai region and it is located in the serene and peaceful town of Tiruchengode, Namakkal.  We have grown in line with a scintillating and spacious campus to dispense a first class education to a wide range of students who are with us and provide ample opportunities for students to nurture the innate qualities.

Mission

SKV Schools will provide its students with an education by nurturing self-discipline, self-confidence and creativity which will encourage in them an enduring love of knowledge and the passion and sense of improvement, as well as responsibility to make a positive difference in the lives of others through their own actions and example.
